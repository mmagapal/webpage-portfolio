ABOUT ME

My initiation as a web developer was probably like many people from my generation. It began when myspace was still around if you don't remember or you don't know, with a myspace profile you could add your own HTML and also style it in any way you wanted and if you didn't know anything about HTML code there were hundreds of pages on the internet that generated code for myspace anything from tables, FLASH MP3 players, slide shows, glowing text, glittery text and even make your profile rain with any symbol haha, overall myspace was sort-of like a combination of Facebook + square space + Spotify, I found so many cool independent artists there that later became mainstream, it was pretty cool. I remember a lot of my high school friends never got their way with myspace, it was a little complicated for them I think and that's when they ask me for help and sometimes even pay me to make their profiles look cool.

After graduating high school I landed a job in finances as an Analyst that allowed me to go to college and finished a degree in Nutrition and Food science but my passion for the internet and technology persisted and I kept learning coding, after three years working as an analyst I lost my job but I got my first job as a coder in a startup company that a friend of mine was starting, since then I kept working on bettering my skills and now I'm working on the switch from being a Web Developer to be a Software Engineer.
